Projects

  • Pan-Cancer Classification

    Research Projects

  • Wi-Fi Crowdsensing Attendance System

    Research Projects

    Research on Wi-Fi Received Signal Strength (RSS)-based indoor localization for automated attendance management.

  • Spam Email Classifier

    Research Projects

    Developed a hybrid metaheuristic optimization model combining Genetic Algorithm (GA) and Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO) under a competitive coevolution framework for feature selection.

  • Apple Leaf Disease Classification
